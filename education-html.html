Pictured is the Loyola Greenhouse. I spent two years working as the Aquaponics Manager overseeing our greenhouse and aquaponics system.
This is the Institue of Environmental Sustainability where I received my degree in Environmental Science. IES is a brand new institute in the campus and I was able to be part of the intital growth and implementation of the curriculum and programs at IES.
Studying at Loyola, I worked on projects and programs that had me both working with professionals in the field and also educating others about the research that I was doing during my studies.
